Our Story

When we first moved into our new university, we faced a lot of issues acclamatizing to the conditions around. Physically and mentally, a move is always a tough thing. More over, moving in had to be with the right people and even after we found them, we still had to search for a chair to sit on and a bed to sleep on!

That's when we started thinking about MarketHub™.Why not connect everyone on campus to enable trading and renting of commodities which may not be useful to some, but could be really helpful to others! We hope you make the most of it! Meet new people and get in touch over the instant messenger! Cheers!
